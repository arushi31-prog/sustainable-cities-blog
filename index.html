Key Features of Sustainable Cities

Renewable Energy Integration: Use of solar, wind, and smart grids.
Efficient Public Transport: Electric buses, cycling lanes, walkable roads.
Green Buildings: Energy-saving designs and eco-friendly materials.
Water Management: Rainwater harvesting and wastewater recycling.
Waste Reduction: Composting, recycling, and waste-to-energy systems.
Smart Tech: AI, IoT, and digital governance for city efficiency.

Smart Urban Planning Strategies

  • Mixed-Use Development: Reduces commute and boosts convenience.
  • Transit-Oriented Development: Metro and BRT-focused planning.
  • Compact City Design: Prevents sprawl and improves efficiency.
  • Green Infrastructure: Parks, rainwater systems, and eco-buildings.
  • Community Involvement: Inclusive planning for better results.

Conclusion

Sustainable cities and smart urban planning are the future of urban development. With green technologies, inclusive strategies, and smart infrastructure, cities can thrive while protecting the environment and improving quality of life.
