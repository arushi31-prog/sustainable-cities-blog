Sustainable Cities and Smart Urban Planning

Introduction

As urban populations grow and environmental challenges intensify, the need for sustainable cities and smart urban planning has never been greater. Sustainable cities aim to balance economic development, social inclusivity, and environmental conservation, ensuring a high quality of life for current and future generations.

Through smart urban planning, cities integrate innovative solutions to manage resources efficiently, reduce carbon footprints, and create resilient, livable communities...
